Featured Games

Free Fire

The gameplay in Free Fire is centered around a battle royale format, where players are pitted against each other in a fight to be the last person standing

Candy Crush

Candy Crush Saga is a "match three" game, where the core gameplay is based on swapping two adjacent candies among several on the gameboard to make a row or column of at least three matching-colored candies.

PUBG

PUBG is a player versus player shooter game in which up to one hundred players fight in a battle royale, a type of large-scale last man standing deathmatch where players fight to remain the last alive. Players can choose to enter the match solo, duo, or with a small team of up to four people.
